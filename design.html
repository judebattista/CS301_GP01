Curse Your Sudden But Inevitable Betrayal

Our game features a dinosaur in a wilderness setting. Thanks to the Lockian nature of the universe, the dinosaur and the human inhabitants establish an atmosphere of mutual respect as the underpinnings of burgeoning trust and friendship. The player's goal as the dinosaur is to strengthen this incipient bond leading to the collective success of the dinosaur/city symbiosis. Note: this feature is not yet implemented. In this prototype, the dinosaur just stomps around and breaks sh stuff. Hobbes was right!

Planned features:

  • Wilderness as backdrop.
  • The dinosaur attempts to destroy vehicles, people, and aircraft for points.
  • The dinosaur has three attacks available: bite, stomp, and tail whip.
  • The dinosaur can destroy some environmental objects with its attacks.
  • Destroying some objects creates health pickups, allowing the dinosaur to survive longer and create more mayhem.
  • Potential Threats
    • Meteors
    • Other dinosaurs
    • Missiles and Aircraft - aerial threats
    • Environmental hazards - EXPLOSIONS?!
  • Background
    • Dimensions: 3000 x 550 px
    • Back layer: sky and clouds
    • Mid layer: mountains
    • Front layer: path and interactive objects
